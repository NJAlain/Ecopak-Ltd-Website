About ECOPAK LTD

Leading in eco-friendly packaging solutions for a sustainable future

Who We Are

ECOPAK LTD is a Rwandan-based company focused on producing high-quality, sustainable packaging solutions using biodegradable materials.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the environmental impact of packaging by offering biodegradable alternatives to plastic. We create durable and versatile jute bags for various uses.

What We Do

At ECOPAK LTD, we use modern production techniques to manufacture jute bags that meet local and global standards. Our eco-friendly packaging solutions range from bulk packaging to customized gifting options.
